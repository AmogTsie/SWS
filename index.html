About Us

Specialist Waste Services (Pty) Ltd is a 100% black female
owned and managed waste Management Company that provides
waste management solutions and services through the best
environment friendly practices.Established in Mokopane,
Limpopo province with a branch in Gauteng.

Values

Dedication

Our Vision

At Specialist Waste Services (Pty) Ltd, we believe that coming together is a beginning; keeping together is progress; working together is success. Our vision is to become the preferred waste management company for the district and to develop a national brand that will partner with our stakeholders and industry to manage & reduce waste from collection to disposal.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be our client's most valued service provider by ensuring success and exceeding expectations on quality, price and delivery schedule. We will comply with local and national laws and do business in an environmentally friendly manner within an integrated waste management framework. To maintain a steady but profitable growth based on our core values by providing value for money to the clients and industry that we serve through our staff.

Services

Manage your waste and decrease your environmental impact, with our range of waste solutions and waste related equipment supply including:

  • Rubble and construction waste
  • Clinical and related waste.
  • Pharmaceutical waste.
  • Regulated and prescribed industrial waste.
  • Commercial and industrial waste.
  • Street/Road Cleaning
  • Tinkered waste services
  • Liquid waste disposal
  • Free waste audits
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) Waste, gloves, masks, wipes and cleaning cloths.

Other services:


  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) products, safety helmets, gloves, eyeglasses, earplugs, Full-body suits, vests, hard hats, safety footwear, and respiratory protective equipment (RPE).
  • Cleaning and disinfection
